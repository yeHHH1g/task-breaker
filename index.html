How it works

Three steps to getting unstuck

01

Describe your task

Type what feels overwhelming. "Clean the apartment", "Write my essay", "Start that project".

02

Get a breakdown

Task Breaker splits it into 6-12 small steps with time estimates and difficulty levels.

03

Check them off

Work through each step. Watch the progress bar fill. Get confetti when you're done.

Stop overthinking. Start doing.
